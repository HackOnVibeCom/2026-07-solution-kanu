# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

Fanfare takes four inputs — app name, a one-line description, target user, and store link — and generates launch copy across four channels: an App Store listing (title, subtitle, keywords); social posts for X, LinkedIn, and a short-form video hook; a Product Hunt-style launch draft; and landing page hero copy. Everything is returned as one structured launch package, revealed section by section with a one-click copy button on each.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Solo and small-team indie mobile app developers who have just shipped or are about to ship, and need launch copy fast without hiring a copywriter or marketer.

**3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?**

English-speaking markets first: United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Nigeria, and India, since these have the highest concentration of indie, hackathon, and solo developer communities.

**4. Who are your competitors?**

Generic AI writing tools like Copy.ai or Jasper, used broadly for any copywriting task. Fanfare is narrower and purpose-built only for the app launch moment, needing no prompting or setup.

**5. What is your advantage?**

Zero setup, one form, one click. Competitors require prompting skill and multiple separate requests to cover a full launch. Fanfare treats the launch as one complete package and returns everything at once, tailored to the specific app.
